Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2, 2025

Overview

At DocuBox, safeguarding your privacy is our foremost commitment. This Privacy Policy explains how we manage and protect your data when you use the DocuBox mobile application.

Data Collection

DocuBox does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not engage in:

  • Analytics tracking
  • User behavior tracking
  • Advertising identifier collection
  • User registration processes

Network Connections (SMB)

If you opt to add an SMB (Server Message Block) server, your credentials are securely stored within the application. Access to your files occurs solely upon your initiation of the connection. No server data or credentials are transmitted to DocuBox or any third party.

Local Files & Security

Your files are never uploaded or synchronized unless you explicitly connect to a network drive. Face ID locking is optional and designed exclusively to protect local folders on your device. No biometric data is ever transmitted off your device.
