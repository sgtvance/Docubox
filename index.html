iOS File Manager

Full control over your documents.

DocuBox is a powerful and intuitive file manager for iOS. Organize files with a unique inbox-to-folder system, lock sensitive folders with Face ID, stream media directly, and connect to your SMB server for seamless access across devices.

Features

📥

Inbox to Folder

A unique filing system that keeps incoming files organized and moves them exactly where you need them.

🔒

Face ID Locking

Lock individual folders with Face ID. Your biometric data never leaves your device.

🎬

Media Streaming

Stream video and audio directly within the app, including WebM support, without transferring files.

🖧

SMB Integration

Connect to your home server or NAS over SMB for seamless access to your files across all devices.

Built for power users.

DocuBox is designed for developers, power users, and anyone who wants more from their iOS file manager. No cloud required, no tracking, no subscriptions — just a clean, capable tool that puts you in control of your files.
